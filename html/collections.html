88rising x Coachella NFT Collections

HEAD IN THE CLOUDS FOREVER

We’re launching three exclusive NFT collections full of digital collectibles and opportunities for unique in-person experiences at the festival.

Own a piece of 88rising’s historic Head in the Clouds Forever performance featuring the visuals by Chinese multidisciplinary artist Curry Tian, watch the show live from a private viewing area, access 88rising’s exclusive Cloud Mansion, and play the first ever NFT scavenger hunt at Coachella.

Curry Tian’s collections are a surreal visual statement of eternal optimism, duality and a belief in the boundless power of Head in the Clouds Asian music culture.

These NFTs will be redeemable for digital and physical experiences at the festival plus tons of amazing perks and utilities from 88rising and Coachella including dedicated entry lines, tickets to Head In The Clouds Festival, an onsite festival meetup, early access to merch and NFT drops, and more.

A portion of the proceeds of the HEAD IN THE CLOUDS FOREVER Collection will be donated to Asian Mental Health Institute. The collaboration with 88rising is a continuation of Coachella’s environmentally friendly NFT marketplace built by FTX US using Solana and Metaplex.
